All the Small Businesses — About Us

All the Small Businesses, established in November 2022, is a product of the M&T Meet & Hack Hackathon that was hosted at Rochester Institute of Technology in November 2022. The idea arose when a team of college students wanted to know how many small businesses were local to their area. With the growing desire to support small businesses, we wanted to know where we could find all of the local owned coffee shops, clothing stores, restaurants, and other businesses that are near any specified general location. With the help of mentors, friends, and Stack Overflow, we were able to bring our idea to fruition.
